Empowering students with information

The PSU Urdaneta Guidance Office Information Service (GoIS) aims to to enhance the quality of student services, streamline the issuance of certifications, and improve the overall efficiency of the guidance office.

Services offered

Admission and Testing Services

It helps screen college freshmen, administering admission, personality, mental ability, and aptitude tests.

Learn more

Information Service

It provides info for student adjustment and decision-making, including orientation, academic regulations, and field specialization, with an emphasis on accurate and valid information.

Learn more

Counseling Service

It fosters interpersonal development through dynamic counseling, with teacher involvement and group/peer counseling.

Learn more
